== Installation ==

1. Upload unziped zomato.zip to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Open widget section and get our widget.
4. Add it to your desired section in your blog.
5. For any queries email at support@zomato.com
