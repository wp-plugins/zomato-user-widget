A simple widget that enables you to flaunt your Zomato foodie stats!

== Description ==
Get the Zomato widget and share your foodie journey on Zomato with everyone who visits your blog or website! Flaunt those gorgeously filtered photos, the delicious reviews you\'ve written, and of course, your loyal foodie fan following.

= Available Languages =
* English
* Indonesian
* Turkish
* Portuguese

= Technical Support =
* Have an issue? Shoot us a mail at [support@zomato.com](mailto:support@zomato.com)

== Installation ==
1. Upload unzipped `zomato.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the \"Plugins\" menu in WordPress.
3. Open widget section and get our widget.
4. Add it to your desired section in your blog.
